What brings about our formation?

Unique College of Management, Science and Technology (UCoMSaT) is a registered institution established as Unique Enterprise in the year 2014 in a way to empower young people and to combat the challenges in purchasing stationery. As a learning institution, its gears towards the empowerment and capacity building of the youth in computer knowledge in order to increase the human capital development in Information technology and other opportunities within the Western Area Rural District and Sierra Leone at large. The said institution is situated at 31 Main Motor Road, Freetown-Masiaka Highway, Waterloo and has been working with the said name after the CEO and General Manager in the person of Idrissa Sankoh.
